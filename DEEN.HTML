Our Story

The Spanish Soul

Located in the heart of New York City, Verde NYC brings the vibrant spirit of Spanish dining to the modern world. We believe that dining is not just about food—it is about the "Sobremesa," the precious time spent talking after a meal.

Our chefs blend traditional recipes with a touch of NYC luxury, creating dishes that are both nostalgic and exciting.
